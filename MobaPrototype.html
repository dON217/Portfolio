MOBA Prototype

About the Project

In this prototype I explored the methods to create basic controls and mechanics from a MOBA-like game. From a Top Down view and mouse clicks for guiding the character movement. Also using the mouse to move the camera around in combination with the arrow keys.

Prototype done in Unreal Engine 5.3

Intial Objectives of the Project

  • Generate a fast prototype of a MOBA-like movement and camera control.
  • Implementation of basic auto attacks.

    • Results and observations

  • Movement and camera control was implemented successfully, and with modularity in mind to adjust several values, such as the camera rate of movement or zoom speeds.
  • Additionally a basic zoom was incorporated to the controls as well as a panning functionality.
  • Locking the camera to the character as it moves around was straightforward to implement as well as jumping to the position of the character without locking in.
  • In the proposed time frame to develop the prototype a very primitive attack system was added. Although faulty in some areas and with room to improve, the character is able to attack certain NPCs given its auto attack range.
